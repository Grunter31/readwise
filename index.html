The five levels of comprehension

Reading is not one skill. It's five — and most children are missing three of them.

Decoding apps teach Level 1. ReadWise builds all five — the ones that determine whether your child succeeds at school and beyond.

Level 1
Finding Facts
Can they find what the text directly says? Most apps stop here.
Level 2
Word Power
Can they use context to understand words they've never seen before?
Level 3
Reading Between the Lines
Can they infer what the author means but doesn't say directly?
Level 4
Big Ideas
Can they identify the main message or theme of a piece?
Level 5
Critical Thinking
Can they evaluate, challenge, and form their own reasoned view?
