The five levels of comprehension

Reading is not one skill. It is five — and most children are only being taught the first.

Popular reading apps focus on decoding — Level 1. ReadWise diagnoses and builds all five. The ones schools actually test.

Level 1
Finding Facts
Can they find what the text directly says? Most apps stop here.
Level 2
Word Power
Can they work out what an unfamiliar word means from the context around it?
Level 3
Reading Between the Lines
Can they understand what the author means but doesn't say directly?
Level 4
Big Ideas
Can they identify the main message or theme of a piece of writing?
Level 5
Critical Thinking
Can they form their own view, explain it, and back it up?
